My Story Starts Here...

My First Job...

"Part-time sales consultant" - That was my first position in 2014. By 2018, I had worked my way up the ladder to managing a flagship store. I learnt so much about sales, customer experience, management and people that I believed I was ready for something more challenging. Based on my belief, I decided to go study Marketing and stepped down to a part-time sales consultant again. Crazy right? hahahha... It only gets crazier!

Student to Star

So my new schedule started in 2019 with a full time job and full time studies. Quite stressful to be honest but I quickly adapted. I learned how to be efficient and plan. What I enjoyed most about studying was not the information that I learned but how this information could spark ideas for innovation and creativity to impact the world postively. As the years of my studies went by, I soon realised that impacting the world positively is something I am deeply passionate about. Inspired by this passion, I worked hard and learnt all I could in Marketing to be able to impact the world positively. I graduated with a summa cum laude in 2022.

Fast Forward

I am currently being trained by Google Experts to become a great UX/UI Designer. I have realised that I absolutely love building useful things to solve real-world problems whilst applying my creativity. I aspire to impact the world positively and I believe becoming a great UX Designer will help me do that.
